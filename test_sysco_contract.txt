FOOD SUPPLY AGREEMENT

This Supply Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Sysco Corporation
("Supplier") and Small Cafe LLC ("Buyer").

PAYMENT TERMS AND INVOICING
Payment shall be due Net 60 days from invoice date. All invoices must be
submitted within 30 days of delivery. Late payments will incur interest
charges at 1.5% per month.

PRICING AND PRICE ADJUSTMENT
Prices are subject to adjustment based on the Consumer Price Index (CPI)
and market conditions. Supplier reserves the right to increase prices with
30 days written notice. Price escalation may occur quarterly.

INDEMNIFICATION
Supplier shall indemnify and hold harmless Buyer from and against all
third-party claims, damages, losses, and expenses arising from product
defects, food safety issues, or breach of food safety regulations.

LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Supplier's total liability under this Agreement shall be limited to the
amount paid by Buyer in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.
Consequential damages are excluded.

AUDIT AND INSPECTION RIGHTS
Buyer shall have the right to audit Supplier's facilities, records, and
food safety procedures at any time with 48-hour notice. Supplier must
maintain records for minimum 3 years.

DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY
Supplier agrees to comply with all applicable data protection laws including
GDPR for any customer data processed. Supplier must maintain appropriate
security measures and report any data breaches within 24 hours.

SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
Supplier guarantees 99% on-time delivery and maintains food safety
certifications including HACCP and ISO 22000. Response time for urgent
orders is 4 hours.

TERMINATION FOR CONVENIENCE
Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience with 90 days
written notice. Termination fee of $25,000 applies if terminated before
24 months.

FORCE MAJEURE
Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to acts of God,
pandemic, epidemic, natural disasters, or government restrictions.

GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
All disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration under AAA rules.

CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of pricing, customer lists,
and proprietary information. Non-disclosure obligations survive termination
for 5 years.

COMPLIANCE WITH LAWS
Supplier warrants compliance with all food safety regulations, FDA
requirements, USDA standards, anti-bribery laws, and FCPA regulations.
